Virtual Reality

Why VR in Training

Various industries such as: Corporates, Healthcare, Manufacturing, etc & academia can leverage the VR platform. VR opens up several opportunities either by enhancing the current training modules or by providing training earlier not feasible due to several reasons:

High retention

In the interactive & immersive environment, people learn by doing things rather than learning passively. This leads to higher retention of the skills learned through VR.

Simulating real experiences

VR allows to simulate the real life scenarios which otherwise is not possible providing the opportunity to train people for actual situations they may face during their work.

Cost savings

In many cases, providing training to employees becomes very costly due to various reasons such as remote locations, event organisation, etc. In VR one can easily simulate these environments leading to significant reduction in cost.

Data analytics

VR allows to track the trainee’s movements within VR environment providing the automated feedback on the performance of task based on set evaluation parameters.

Making learning fun

VR makes the training fun increasing the participation level of trainees. Get over with the traditional mode of imparting training through lectures, presentations, videos etc.
